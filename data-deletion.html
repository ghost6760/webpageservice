Data Deletion Instructions

How to request deletion of your data from Hachi

Your Privacy Matters: At Hachi, we respect your right to have your personal data deleted. This page provides clear instructions on how to request deletion of your data collected through our services, including interactions via Facebook Messenger, WhatsApp, and Instagram.

How to Request Data Deletion

If you wish to delete your data from our systems, please follow these simple steps:

  1. Send an email to our Data Protection team
    Email address: ceo@hachi.live
  2. Include the following information in your email:
    • Subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
    • Your full name
    • Email address or phone number associated with your account
    • Platform(s) where you interacted with us (Messenger, WhatsApp, Instagram, Website)
    • Your User ID (if known) or any other identifying information
  3. Wait for confirmation
    We will process your request within 30 days and send you a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request data deletion, we will remove the following information from our systems:

Personal Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Profile information

Communication Data

  • Message history
  • Conversation logs
  • Interaction records
  • Media files shared

Service Data

  • Appointment history
  • Service requests
  • Preferences
  • Usage history

Processing Timeline

We take data deletion requests seriously and process them as quickly as possible:

  • Acknowledgment: We will acknowledge receipt of your request within 3 business days
  • Processing: Your data will be deleted within 30 days of receiving your request
  • Confirmation: You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete

Important Notes

Please be aware:
  • Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • Some data may be retained for legal compliance purposes (e.g., financial records, legal obligations)
  • Anonymized or aggregated data used for analytics may be retained
  • If you have active appointments or services, they may be cancelled
  • You will need to create a new account if you wish to use our services again

Platform-Specific Deletion

Facebook Messenger

To delete data collected through Messenger, send us a deletion request via email. You can also manage your Facebook data through your Facebook account settings.

WhatsApp

To delete data collected through WhatsApp Business, send us a deletion request via email. You can also delete your chat history directly in WhatsApp.

Instagram

To delete data collected through Instagram, send us a deletion request via email. You can also manage your Instagram data through your Instagram account settings.

Alternative Methods

If you prefer, you can also request data deletion by:

  • Website: Visit https://hachi.live and contact us through the contact form
  • Direct Message: Send a message to our business account on the platform where you interacted with us, requesting data deletion

If you have any questions about the data deletion process or need assistance, please contact us:

Email: ceo@hachi.live
